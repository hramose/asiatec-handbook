2.How to compute the General Average Grade?


Answer:

Prelim Exam (40%) + Prelim Class Standing (60%)

= Prelim Grade (100%)


Midterm Exam (40%) + Midterm Class Standing (60%)

= Midterm Grade (100%)


Pre final Exam (40%) + Pre final Class Standing (60%)

= Pre final Grade (100%)


Final Exam (40%) + Final Class Standing (60%)

= Final Grade (100%)


Prelim + Midterm + Pre final + Final = General Average

Question:

9. What is the Refund Policy of ASIATECH?


Answer:

A student who transfers or withdraws in writing within two (2) weeks after the beginning of the classes and who has already paid the prescribed tuition and other school fees in full or/for any length longer than one month, shall be charged twenty percent (20%) or if the student withdraws within the first week of classes, ten percent (10%) of the total amount due for the term. Student shall be charged all the school fees in full if he/she withdraws after the second week of classes.

10. What are the Guidelines on Course Dropping?


Answer:

The following procedure will guide the students in the dropping of courses: Secure a dropping form from the Registrar's Office. See the faculty concerned to seek his consent/advice and signature. Proceed to the Cahier for payment. Submit the accomplished dropping form to the Registrar's Office.
